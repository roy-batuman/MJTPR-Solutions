Why Zonta Naples?

We’re a community of professionals and advocates dedicated to advancing the status of women and girls in Collier County. Through hands-on service, scholarships, and advocacy, we support safety, education, and economic opportunity—while connecting members to Zonta’s global network.

What members do

  • Support local service projects and volunteer days
  • Participate in advocacy to end violence against women and girls
  • Help fund and select scholarship recipients
  • Build leadership skills and expand your professional network

How membership works

  1. Apply: Complete the application (PDF) and email it to the Membership Chair.
  2. Visit: Join us for a meeting to learn about projects and committees.
  3. Engage: Choose where you would like to serve—events, advocacy, fundraising, more.
  4. Induct: Finalize dues and club onboarding.
