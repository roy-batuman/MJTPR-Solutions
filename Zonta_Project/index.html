Who We Are

The Zonta Club of Naples is part of a global organization dedicated to empowering women through service and advocacy. Our members come from diverse backgrounds but share a passion for improving our communities and supporting women worldwide.

Scholarships

We award scholarships to women pursuing higher education and leadership development. Our scholarship programs focus on empowering women in STEM, business, and community leadership roles.

  • Young Women in STEM: $5,000 annual award
  • Leadership Development: $3,000 annual award
  • Community Impact: $2,500 annual award

Membership

Become a member and join a network of inspiring women dedicated to service and advocacy. Members gain access to leadership opportunities, networking events, and the chance to make a tangible impact in the community.
