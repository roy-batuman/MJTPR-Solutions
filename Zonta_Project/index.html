Empowering Women, Creating Change

The Zonta Club of Naples is dedicated to building a better world for women and girls. Through our community outreach, scholarship programs, and partnerships with local organizations, we create opportunities for education, leadership, and empowerment. Our members are professionals and advocates who believe in service and equality for all. Together, we work to end violence against women and uplift those in need — making an impact that lasts for generations.
