Zonta Club of Naples

Zonta Club of Naples is part of tens of thousands of professionals worldwide demanding gender equality and an end to violence against women and girls. We eradicate human trafficking, domestic violence, and child marriage. Zonta provides grants, scholarships, and resources while advocating change and educating the community.

Empowering Women in Our Community

The Zonta Club of Naples is dedicated to building a better world for women and girls through community outreach, scholarships, and partnerships. Our members believe in service and equality for all.

Together, we work to end violence and uplift those in need, making an impact that lasts generations.

Who We Are

The Zonta Club of Naples is a group of professional women working to advance the status of women locally and globally. We advocate for gender equality, fight violence against women, and support educational opportunities through scholarships and mentorship.

Our mission is rooted in service, advocacy, and empowerment, making lasting change in our community and beyond.

Scholarships

We provide scholarships to women pursuing higher education, particularly in fields where women are underrepresented. Our goal is to empower future leaders, innovators, and changemakers.

Scholarship applications are reviewed by our committee, and awards are based on merit, leadership, and commitment to community service.
